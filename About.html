About Me:

I'm a proud Ontarian, born and raised in Canada. I have a diverse range of interests spanning from aviation, technology, and artificial intelligence to gaming, particularly in the world of Roblox. I'm also passionate about graphic design, business and law. I have average/surface level knowledge of CSS and HTML through my time using Stylus along with Fandom (and a Coding and Animations Class during High School.)


In my free time, you'll find me creating Stylus Themes, gaming, collaborating on a community-led and maintained encylopedia for a Roblox experience (PTFS Wiki), along with contributing to discussions on the Roblox DevForums.


My pronouns are He/Him and They/Them, and I identity as pansexual.
